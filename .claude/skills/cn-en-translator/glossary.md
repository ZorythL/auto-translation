## Style decisions
- Pinyin names, family-name-first order, no tone marks.
- Period register: small-town mainland China; keep ration-era texture.
- Dialogue in English “double quotes”; sound natural and spoken.
- "James" stays English (source uses it); all other names pinyin.
- Censored "□□" in source → "[redacted]".

## Known inconsistencies to reconcile (first-pass artifacts)
The first pass ran 13 parallel translators, so a few names introduced *after* chapter 1 may vary slightly between chapter batches (e.g. 玉香樓 as "Restaurant" vs "Tower"). For a clean release, do a consistency polish pass across `english-chapters/` using this glossary as the authority.
