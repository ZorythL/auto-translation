**Title:** Yao Yuan's Trade in Feelings

A modern Chinese women's-fiction drama: Yao Yuan is a celebrity "emotional consultant" / livestreamer who treats relationships as a business of value exchange. Woven through it is a time-travel ("穿越") thread driven by an ancient bronze mirror and a supernatural "Mirror Pact" (鏡契), under which her trips through time become another man's good fortune.
