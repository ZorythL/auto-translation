Her tone stayed calm, but her words came a little faster, betraying a wound that had never fully healed. "I was a wreck for a whole year. Later I steeled myself, decided to channel all my energy into myself—signed up for courses, built up a client base, and slowly grew this company into what it is now. Life is a game of chess, and so are feelings. Beyond the window, the sun had begun to slant westward, dyeing the Yellow River's waters a deeper amber dusk.
